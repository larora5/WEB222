About the Creator

My name is Lakshay Arora, and I am a passionate pool player and web developer. I created Pool World to share my love for this timeless game with others and provide a platform for pool enthusiasts to learn more about the game and share their experiences.

After playing pool for many years, I have come to appreciate its unique blend of strategy, skill, and social interaction. I chose to create this website because I wanted to share my passion for the game and help others discover the joy it can bring.
